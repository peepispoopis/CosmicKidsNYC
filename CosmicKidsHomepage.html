Cosmic Kids NYC is committed to interactive learning, giving children the opportunity to explore on the web and in their own backyard.

We wanted children to be able to have knowledge of what is like being an astronaut through thelive International Space Station feed.

Learning about the Planetary Orbits to understand what it means in the physical world when people say Mercury is in retrograde.

Knowing the time of the sunrise and sunset in a child's location can give them more spatial and environmental awareness, prompting to go outside and watch the sunrise or sunset.
