The team members of Cosmic Kids NYC are all current students at Pratt Institute's School of Information, where they are engaged in the study of innovative and cutting-edge technologies in the field of information science. At the School of Information, they are part of a vibrant academic community that emphasizes hands-on learning and real-world application. With access to state-of-the-art facilities and a faculty of renowned scholars and practitioners, they are gaining valuable skills in areas such as digital archives, information management, human-computer interaction, and emerging technologies. Their education is enriched by connections to New York City's dynamic professional networks, providing them with unparalleled opportunities to work with top organizations in the rapidly expanding fields of information and technology.
